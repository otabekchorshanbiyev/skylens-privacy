Weather · Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how Otabek Chorshanbiyev ("I", "me", or "my") handles information in the SkyLens weather application. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about data practices.
📍

1. Information We Collect

SkyLens collects only the minimum data necessary to provide weather information.

  • Location Data — Your device's GPS coordinates are used to fetch local weather data. Location is only accessed when the app is in use and is never stored on our servers.
  • City Search Queries — When you manually search for a city, the city name is sent to the OpenWeatherMap API to retrieve weather data.
⚙️

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Display current weather conditions for your location
  • Show hourly and 7-day weather forecasts
  • Display weather maps and radar layers
  • Show sunrise and sunset times
🌐

3. Third-Party Services

SkyLens uses the following third-party services to function:

  • OpenWeatherMap API — Weather data provider. Your location coordinates or city name are sent to their servers. View their privacy policy at openweathermap.org/privacy-policy
  • OpenStreetMap — Map tiles provider for the map screen. View their privacy policy at osmfoundation.org
🔒

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

✅  We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity

✅  We do not store your location history

✅  We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs

✅  We do not share your data with any third parties beyond what is necessary to fetch weather data

✅  We do not sell your data

💾

5. Data Storage & Security

SkyLens does not operate its own servers. All weather data is fetched directly from OpenWeatherMap and displayed in real-time. No personal data is stored on external servers by this application. Any cached data is stored locally on your device only and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

📌

6. Location Permission

SkyLens requests access to your device's location for the sole purpose of showing local weather. Specifically:

  • Location is accessed only while the app is open (When In Use)
  • Location coordinates are sent directly to OpenWeatherMap to fetch weather data
  • You can deny location permission and manually search for a city instead
  • Location data is never stored or logged by this application
👶

7. Children's Privacy

SkyLens does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account registration or any personal data submission to function.

📝

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
